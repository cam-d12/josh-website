Two in a row silences the mischievous 
            silence gives up its quiet act
            as if you’ve been here before: rip
            studded heels from glistening mud send
            a body forwards not more
            towards everything
            Is up-side
            down; w/ the life
            of the bat the crowd will be roaring
            turning violently everything’s up side down
            “it can’t be” repeated
            
            
            J’shua
